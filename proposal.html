Group AA1 Project Proposal: Fashion Sustainability Web Application

Problem & Data Domain

A common passion and interest all of our team members found that we shared is fashion, specifically fashion sustainability. In contrast to fast fashion, sustainable fashion is a term that is used to describe fashion that is both ethically made and environmentally friendly. This means that the clothing garments that are created reduces the impact on the environment and is mindful of the workers who produce these clothing garments. There are a number of issues with fast fashion, such as water pollution, waste management, greenhouse gas emissions, and more. People can also be unaware that they are actually consumers of fast fashion, or they don't know what alternate options there are out there and don't know where to start. Our team wants to create a shopping website that promotes and encourages partaking in sustainable fashion.

In general, most people are unaware of how the fashion industry pollutes the world. They are also unaware of affordable sustainable clothing. One existing solution to this problem would be to look up the most sustainable brands. However, this list is not currated to the person's style or price range. Fast fashion is also accompanied by poor working conditions, results in carbon emissons, and countless other harmful environmental effects.

App Description

  • The primary users of the application will be working age young adults, between the ages of 18-30. We also want to make sure to accomodate those in a lower socio-economic bracket by providing brands that are sustainable and affordable.
  • One feature of our app would be a searching capability to find sustainable brands that sell the desired product. Another capabilty would be to sort through categories of different styled clothes. The user could select a style, such as "nigh out", and the site would reccomend sustainable brand options. The last feature we will add is the ability to sort by price range.
  • The users will be viewing lists of sustainable fashion companies.
  • This app works to combat the lack of information surrounding fast fashion alternatives. We want to provide young people with the option to buy the clothes they desire from companies that are fair to employees and good for the environment.
